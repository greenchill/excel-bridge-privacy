# Privacy Policy - Excel Bridge for Jira

**Last updated: January 2026**

## Overview

Excel Bridge for Jira ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data when you use our Atlassian Forge application.

## Data Collection

### What we access:
- **Jira Issues**: The App reads and writes issues in your Jira projects to perform import/export operations
- **Project Information**: Project keys and names for the project selector
- **User Information**: Current user context for "My Issues" filter

### What we store:
- **Import Session Data**: Temporarily stored in Forge Storage for rollback functionality (auto-deleted after 30 days)
- **Field Mapping Templates**: Saved configurations for reuse (stored in your Jira instance via Forge Storage)
- **Sync History**: Records of import/export operations (last 100 entries)

### What we DO NOT collect:
- Personal identification information
- Email addresses
- Payment information
- Usage analytics
- Data from other Atlassian products

## Data Processing

- All data processing happens within Atlassian's Forge infrastructure
- Your data never leaves Atlassian's servers
- We do not transmit data to external servers
- Uploaded CSV/Excel files are processed in-memory and not stored

## Data Security

- The App runs on Atlassian Forge, which provides enterprise-grade security
- All API calls use Atlassian's secure authentication
- No credentials are stored by the App

## Your Rights

You can:
- Delete sync history from the App's History tab
- Delete saved templates at any time
- Use rollback to remove imported issues
- Uninstall the App to remove all stored data

## Third Parties

The App does not share any data with third parties.
